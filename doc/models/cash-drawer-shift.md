
# Cash Drawer Shift

This model gives the details of a cash drawer shift.
The cash_payment_money, cash_refund_money, cash_paid_in_money,
and cash_paid_out_money fields are all computed by summing their respective
event types.

## Structure

`Cash Drawer Shift`
